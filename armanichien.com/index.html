Every day is a vacation!

Hi, my name is Armani Chien.

I am currently a Systems Developer at Reflection Projections 2022, we use React and Next.js to build the landing page of this annual Midwestern tech conference hosted at U of I by ACM that brings industry and academia together. I'm passionate about technology, entrepreneurship, and sharing my passions to the world.

I have experience in taking charge of projects and keeping everyone including myself accountable being the Lead Event Manager of both our school's student council and a rotary sponsored club, and have taught countless coding classes on Python and Mobile app development in my previous internship at First Code Academy.

Through my academic work, my professional experience, and my own interest, I've learned Python, C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS and gained experience with frameworks such as React and Django, among others. I've also used tools such as SQL, Docker, and GIT.

Beyond the screen, I love to travel with friends, cook delicious food (my instagram), and learn cool (sometimes useless) skills like headstands, crow poses, mnemonic techniques, juggling, pen spinning, photography, massages, digital art and the list goes on.
